## Rule
Do not display personal email addresses (daniel@eidosagi.com) on the public website. Use GitHub links and repo issues for contact.

## Why
Founder preference. Contact flows through GitHub, not inbox.

## Violation Examples
- Adding mailto: links anywhere on the site
- Displaying email in the footer or contact section
