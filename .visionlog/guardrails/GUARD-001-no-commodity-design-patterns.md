## Rule
Never use gradient blobs, AI-generated illustrations, "Trusted by" logo bars, three-column feature grids, Inter font, generic hero patterns (gradient bg + centered text + two buttons), or border-radius > 4px.

## Why
The book's thesis: the AI aesthetic peaked. Commodity patterns signal "we used the same tools as everyone else." The site must look human-made.

## Violation Examples
- Adding a `background: linear-gradient(...)` for decoration
- Using stock AI art anywhere on the site
- Creating a "Features" section with 3 equal columns of icon + heading + text
