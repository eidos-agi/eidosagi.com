## Rule
Copy must be declarative, not persuasive. No marketing jargon, no passive voice, no vague claims. Numbers beat adjectives. State what it does, not why it's amazing.

## Why
The voice is "confident and unhurried." We ship real software. The repos speak for themselves.

## Violation Examples
- "Revolutionize your workflow with AI-powered solutions"
- "Our cutting-edge platform leverages synergies"
- "The most powerful governance toolkit on the market"
