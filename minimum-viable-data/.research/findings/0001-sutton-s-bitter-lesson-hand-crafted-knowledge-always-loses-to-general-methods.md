## Claim

General methods that leverage computation consistently outperform hand-crafted human knowledge as compute scales. Demonstrated in chess (Deep Blue's simple alpha-beta search beat expert-encoded systems), Go (AlphaGo Zero eliminated human expertise entirely, trained by self-play alone), and speech (general hidden Markov models beat 1970s hand-crafted approaches). Skills files are hand-crafted knowledge for AI. Contracts are the general method — they specify the goal and let the model's increasing capability determine the approach.
